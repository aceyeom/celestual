Privacy Policy

Last updated 28 June 2026

Celestual (celestual.us) is an anonymous, reciprocal matching site: you enter your Instagram @ and the @ of someone you’re thinking about, and you only ever find out if it’s mutual. One-sided entries are never revealed to anyone. This policy explains exactly what we collect, how we use it, who processes it, and how you can delete it.

What we collect

  • Instagram handles. When you make an entry we store two handles — yours and the one you enter — so we can detect whether the same pairing is later entered in reverse. These are stored as part of an encrypted entry record.
  • Your email (optional). Only if you provide one, and only so we can notify you if an entry turns out to be mutual, or remind you. You can use Celestual without giving an email.
  • Identity-verification signal. To confirm an @ is really yours, you send a one-time code by Instagram DM to our account (@celestual.us). Meta’s messaging webhook tells our backend that the code arrived from that handle. We do not receive your Instagram password and never gain access to your Instagram account.
  • Basic technical data. Standard request/log data needed to serve the site securely (e.g. handled by our hosting and database providers). We do not run advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

How we use it

  • To compute whether an entry is mutual, and only then notify the earlier entrant.
  • To restore your own stars (“your sky”) when you sign back in with the same @.
  • To enforce a fair, server-side weekly entry budget and prevent abuse.
  • To verify, when required, that an @ belongs to the person using it.

What we never do

  • We never reveal a one-sided entry. There is no on-screen result for the person you entered.
  • We never post, message, follow, or alert the person you enter.
  • We never sell your data or share it for advertising.

Who processes your data

We use a small set of trusted service providers, each only for the function below:

  • Supabase — encrypted database and backend functions that store entries and the optional email.
  • Vercel — hosting and delivery of the website.
  • ManyChat & Meta Platforms (Instagram) — relay the one-time DM verification message and tell us the code was received from your handle.
  • Resend — sends the match / reminder email, when you’ve given one.

How long we keep it

We keep an entry only while it’s needed for the matching service. You can remove your data at any time (see below), and we delete data without undue delay when you ask, when you delete your account, or when it’s no longer needed to provide the service.

Deleting your data

You can delete your data yourself at any time, and you can ask us to erase and block any handle — including your own, if someone entered you. Full, step-by-step instructions are here:

→ How to delete your Celestual data, or email privacy@celestual.us and we’ll handle it for you.

For adults

Celestual is intended for people 18 and older. It is not directed at children.

Changes & contact

We’ll keep this page current as the product grows; material changes will be reflected by the “last updated” date above. Questions or requests: privacy@celestual.us.
