Delete your data

Last updated 28 June 2026

You’re always in control of your Celestual data. There are three ways to delete it — pick whichever you prefer. All of them remove your information from our systems; the email option is here so you never have to depend on the app to get your data erased.

1 Remove & block a handle yourself Fastest

The quickest, self-service path — no account or sign-in needed:

  1. Open celestual.us and go to the privacy & terms page (the “privacy & terms” link on the landing screen).
  2. Scroll to “Remove & block a handle.”
  3. Type the Instagram handle you want erased — including your own — and tap “Remove & block this handle.”

This deletes every entry that references that handle and blocks it from ever being entered again.

2 Delete your whole account

If you’ve signed in and built up a “sky,” open the profile menu (top-left) and choose “Delete”. This erases your saved stars and account data from our database.

3 Email us and we’ll do it for you

Send a message to privacy@celestual.us with the Instagram handle(s) you want removed. So we can act on it quickly, please include the handle in the request. We’ll confirm once it’s done.

What gets deleted

  • Every entry referencing the handle (both directions of any pairing).
  • Your saved “sky” and any account record tied to the handle.
  • Your email address, if you provided one.

How long it takes

Self-service removal (options 1 and 2) takes effect immediately. Email requests are completed without undue delay — normally within a few days and no later than 30 days.

Questions about your data or this process? Email privacy@celestual.us. See also our Privacy Policy.
