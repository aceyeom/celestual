Delete your data

Last updated 3 July 2026

You're always in control of your celestual data — and so is anyone whose handle was ever entered, whether or not they've used celestual. There are three ways to delete it; the email option exists so you never have to depend on the app to get your data erased. Two things also happen without you asking: unmatched pings purge themselves after sixty days, and who a ping points at is stored only as a salted hash we cannot read.

1 Opt a handle out yourself Fastest

The one-step, self-service path — no account or sign-in needed:

  1. Open celestual.us/optout (also reachable from the "opt out" link on the landing screen).
  2. Type the Instagram handle you want erased — including your own.
  3. Tap "opt this handle out, permanently."

This erases everything referencing that handle — pings in both directions, matches, community and campus records — and makes the handle permanently un-pingable. It is free, immediate, and always will be.

2 Erase your whole account

If you've placed pings, open the account chip (top-left) and choose "delete everything." This retires every ping you've placed, erases your records, closes your door page, and blocks your own handle from being entered — the full opt-out, plus a local wipe of this device.

3 Email us and we'll do it for you

Send a message to privacy@celestual.us with the Instagram handle(s) you want removed. Please include the handle in the request so we can act quickly. We'll confirm once it's done.

What gets deleted

  • Every ping referencing the handle (both directions of any pairing, matched or not).
  • Any match records and pending notifications involving the handle.
  • Membership, community ("worlds") and campus-preregistration records tied to the handle.
  • Your email address, if you provided one.

How long it takes

Self-service removal (options 1 and 2) takes effect immediately. Email requests are completed without undue delay — normally within a few days and no later than 30 days. Independently of any request, an unmatched ping is purged automatically sixty days after it was placed or last renewed.

Questions about your data or this process? Email privacy@celestual.us. See also our Privacy Policy.
